About ICAGAS 2027

Advancing AI Research for a Smarter, Autonomous World

About ICAGAS 2027

The International Conference on Agentic and Generative Artificial Intelligence for Advanced Engineering Informatics and Autonomous Systems (ICAGAS 2027) is a premier international forum dedicated to advancing research and innovation at the intersection of Agentic AI, Generative AI, Engineering Informatics, and Autonomous Systems. The conference will be organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST), Tiruchirappalli, India, in hybrid mode, enabling participation from researchers and practitioners worldwide.

ICAGAS 2027 aims to bring together leading academicians, researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and technology innovators to exchange ideas, present cutting-edge research findings, and discuss emerging challenges and opportunities in intelligent autonomous systems. The conference focuses on the development of AI systems capable of autonomous reasoning, decision-making, planning, content generation, and adaptive learning for engineering and industrial applications.

The technical program will cover a broad spectrum of topics, including Agentic AI architectures, Generative AI, Large Language Models, Digital Twins, Autonomous Robotics, Multi-Agent Systems, Human-AI Collaboration, Explainable AI, Cyber-Physical Systems, Smart Manufacturing, Industry 5.0, Intelligent Transportation, Healthcare Engineering, Smart Cities, and Trustworthy AI.

About SRM IST Tiruchirappalli

One of the best universities in India, SRM Institute of Science and Technology (NIRF ranking 18 under the university category, NAAC A++ grade) offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate programmes in six faculties: Engineering & Technology, Management, Medicine & Health Sciences, Science & Humanities, Law, and Agricultural Sciences. Its campuses are located in and around Chennai, Tiruchirappalli (TN), Modinagar (UP), Sonepat (Haryana), Amaravati (AP), and Gangtok (Sikkim).

About the Department of CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Trichy was established in 2021 with the aim of giving standard education to students and helping them reach their full potential. The department provides top-notch IT infrastructure, a world-class environment for learning and research, collaborations with industry leaders, and promotes moral and ethical values.

Paper Submission Instructions

While preparing your manuscript, we recommend using the Springer LNCS/LNAI template. Short papers: 6–11 pages. Long papers: 12–15 pages (approximately 400 words per page).

Review Process

  • ✓All submitted manuscripts will undergo a rigorous full-paper double-blind peer-review process.
  • ✓Screened submissions undergo an initial quality check through plagiarism detection software (similarity index must be below 5%).
  • ✓Manuscripts are then sent for single-blind review by three independent referees.
  • ✓A consensus of at least two of three reviewers is mandatory for acceptance.
  • ✓One reviewer will not review more than three manuscripts in a single cycle.

Explore Tiruchirappalli

A comprehensive overview of Tiruchirappalli's historical heritage, scenic spots, architectural wonders, and cultural backdrop for incoming delegates.

🏛️ Historical Significance

Tiruchirappalli, commonly known as Trichy, has a rich historical legacy dating back over two millennia. Once a prominent stronghold of the ancient Chola dynasty, Trichy flourished magnificently under the subsequent rule of the Nayak dynasty. Iconic landmarks such as the majestic Rockfort Temple and the grand Ranganathaswamy Temple in Srirangam bear timeless testament to the city's ancient heritage, strategic defensive architecture, and breathtaking temple masterworks.

🗺️ Geographical Features

Nestled amidst the scenic beauty of Tamil Nadu, Tiruchirappalli boasts of the majestic Cauvery River flowing through its heart. The city is uniquely characterized by its soaring rocky outcrops and lush greenery, creating a picturesque contrast for its urban landscape. The surrounding agricultural basin contributes significantly to the state's agriculture, with vibrant green rice paddies and banana groves dotting the beautiful countryside.

PRIMARY PLACES TO VISIT

Heritage & Temple

🕉️ Ranganathaswamy Temple

One of the largest functioning Hindu temples in the world, dedicated to Lord Ranganatha (an incarnation of Lord Vishnu). Spread across a massive 156-acre complex with towering architectural gopurams.

Landmark Fort

🏰 Rockfort Temple

A historic fortress built on an 83-meter high ancient rock structure. Houses ancient Pallava-era rock-cut cave shrines and a summit temple offering panoramic views of the entire urban landscape.

Island Heritage

🏝️ Srirangam Island

A picturesque river island bounded by the Cauvery and Kollidam rivers. Home to the legendary temple complex, a critical pilgrimage site in South India, and unique traditional settlements.

Ancient Engineering

🌊 Kallanai Dam

An ancient water-diversion structure built across the Kaveri River by King Karikala Chola in the 2nd century AD. Celebrated globally as one of the oldest functioning irrigation systems.

Divine Architecture

🔱 Jambukeswarar Temple

A historic Shiva temple representing the water element (Pancha Bhoota Stalam). Famous for its majestic architectural corridors and an active fresh spring under the primary deity.

Colonial Monument

⛪ St. Joseph's Church

An elegant, soaring colonial-era Gothic cathedral constructed in 1840. Renowned for its tall spire, intricate stained-glass panels, and serene sanctuary situated in the center of town.

Museum & Art

🏺 Government Museum

A repository of rich anthropological and geological relics. Showcases Pallava and Chola bronzes, ancient stone sculptures, and historic models of the region's cultural past.

Hill Temple

🦚 Viralimalai Murugan Temple

Perched atop a natural rocky hill, this ancient temple dedicated to Lord Murugan is surrounded by a scenic sanctuary populated by peacocks, delivering spiritual and aesthetic peace.

SCENIC & TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

Scenic Reservoir

🌅 Teppakulam

A massive, historically constructed square temple tank with a beautiful central mandapam. Hosts the colorful annual float festival under brilliant lights in the evening.

Nature Park

🦋 Butterfly Park

Spanning 35 acres, this spectacular park features beautiful botanical gardens, walking trails, an active breeding conservatory, and peaceful spots ideal for nature enthusiasts.

Archeological Cave

🎨 Kudumiyanmalai Temple

An ancient rock-cut monument decorated with rare carvings and historical inscriptions, representing ancient South Indian musical systems and early structural craftsmanship.

Natural Valley

🏞️ Puliyancholai Waterfalls

A tranquil stream cascade nestled inside forest valleys at the foot of the Kolli Hills. Perfect for trekking, nature trails, and spending a quiet afternoon away from urban noise.

Recreation Picnic

🌳 Mukkombu (Upper Anaicut)

A scenic dam spot where the Cauvery river forks beautifully. Features manicured riverside parks, children's playgrounds, and cool river breeze making it a popular family spot.

Science & Space

🚀 Anna Science Centre Planetarium

An immersive scientific hub featuring an active planetarium theater, 3D show galleries, hands-on scientific projects, and an interactive park for tech and space enthusiasts.

Sacred Ghat

🪔 Amma Mandapam Bathing Ghat

A historical stone-carved bathing ghat directly lining the Cauvery River. Frequented by pilgrims participating in traditional prayer routines and sacred rituals.

Historic Epigraphy

📝 Rock Inscriptions of Ucchi Pillayar

Intricate, centuries-old royal inscriptions and engravings carved directly along the stone pathways leading to the summit fortress temple, outlining ancient local annals.

🎭 Cultural Heritage

Trichy is a vibrant tapestry of religious diversity and cultural richness. Magnificently structured temples, ancient mosques, and classic Gothic churches coexist harmoniously, reflecting the city's cosmopolitan history. Traditional festivals like Pongal, Diwali, and Eid are celebrated with immense fervor, with colorful local street processions, classic musical performances, and elaborate temple rituals. Trichy's world-class bronze idol sculpture crafts and high-quality handloom silk sarees highlight the profound talent of local artisans.

⚡ Modern Development

Trichy beautifully balances its deep historical roots with rapid technical advancement. Elite academic pillars like the National Institute of Technology (NIT Trichy), IIM Trichy, and Bharathidasan University drive innovation. Strategically located in central Tamil Nadu, the city boasts high-quality transport networks. Trichy International Airport (TRZ) connects travelers to global routes, and its massive central railway hub bridges transit across India.

Destination Summary

Trichy, with its harmonious blend of history, culture, and modernity, offers a captivating tapestry of experiences for visitors and residents alike. Whether exploring ancient temple corridors, lively handloom bazaars, or scenic riverbeds, Trichy inspires all who step foot here.
