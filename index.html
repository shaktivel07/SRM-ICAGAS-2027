Welcome

A Forum Shaping the Future of Autonomous Intelligence

We are proud to announce ICAGAS 2027 — the International Conference on Agentic and Generative Artificial Intelligence for Advanced Engineering Informatics and Autonomous Systems — organised by the School of Computing, SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST), Tiruchirappalli, India.

The conference will be held in hybrid mode on 12–13 February 2027, enabling researchers and practitioners from across the globe to participate in person or virtually. ICAGAS 2027 targets original, high-quality contributions at the intersection of Agentic AI, Generative AI, engineering informatics, and autonomous systems.

Through keynote addresses, technical sessions, workshops, and industry-academia panels, ICAGAS 2027 will provide a dynamic platform for exchanging ideas, forging collaborations, and accelerating the transition of AI research into real-world engineering solutions.

About

About ICAGAS 2027

The International Conference on Agentic and Generative Artificial Intelligence for Advanced Engineering Informatics and Autonomous Systems (ICAGAS 2027) is a premier international forum dedicated to advancing research and innovation at the intersection of Agentic Artificial Intelligence, Generative Artificial Intelligence, Engineering Informatics, and Autonomous Systems.

The conference will be organised by the School of Computing, SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST), Tiruchirappalli, India, in hybrid mode, enabling participation from researchers and practitioners worldwide.

ICAGAS 2027 aims to bring together leading academicians, researchers, industry professionals, policymakers, and technology innovators to exchange ideas, present cutting-edge research findings, and discuss emerging challenges and opportunities in intelligent autonomous systems.

Objectives

Conference Objectives

  • 01

    To promote the dissemination of high-quality research in Agentic AI, Generative AI, Engineering Informatics, and Autonomous Systems.

  • 02

    To foster interdisciplinary collaboration among researchers, industry experts, and practitioners working in intelligent autonomous technologies and advanced engineering applications.

  • 03

    To explore innovative AI-driven solutions for complex challenges in smart manufacturing, robotics, healthcare, transportation, digital twins, cyber-physical systems, and smart cities.

  • 04

    To encourage the development of trustworthy, explainable, secure, and human-centric AI systems capable of autonomous decision-making and adaptive learning.

  • 05

    To provide opportunities for knowledge exchange through keynote lectures, technical sessions, workshops, special tracks, and industry-academia interactions.

  • 06

    To showcase emerging technologies and practical applications that support Industry 5.0, intelligent automation, and sustainable engineering development.

  • 07

    To facilitate networking and research partnerships among international experts, research institutions, industrial organisations, and government agencies.

  • 08

    To identify future research challenges, opportunities, and policy considerations associated with the deployment of Agentic and Generative AI in real-world autonomous systems.

  • 09

    To bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and industrial implementation by highlighting novel methodologies, case studies, and best practices.

  • 10

    To contribute to the advancement of next-generation intelligent systems that are autonomous, resilient, ethical, and beneficial to society.

Submission Guidelines

How to Submit

Step 01

Prepare Manuscript

Format your paper using the specified conference template. Anonymise all author details for double-blind review. Ensure originality — plagiarism check is mandatory.

Step 02

Check Paper Length

Full papers: 6–10 pages (excluding references). Short papers: 4–5 pages. Papers must not be under simultaneous review elsewhere.

Step 03

Select Track

Choose the most appropriate track from the 11 available research tracks. Cross-track submissions should indicate the primary track at the time of submission.

Step 04

Submit via Portal

Upload your PDF via the official submission portal (link to be activated). Include title, abstract (150–250 words), and 4–6 keywords. File size must not exceed 10 MB.
